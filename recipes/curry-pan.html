Curry Pan

Description

Yummy fried Japanese curry pan. Best made with leftover curry.

Curry is the best, eat more curry. This bread can be frozen and easily reheated.

Ingredients

  • 150 g bread flour
  • 50 g cake flour
  • 3 g salt
  • 15 g sugar
  • 3 g yeast
  • 125 ml whole milk
  • 10 g unsalted butter
  • about 300 g leftover curry
  • 1 large egg
  • 60 g panko
  • oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together bread flour, cake flour, kosher salt, and sugar.
  2. Microwave the milk until warm to the touch. Then add in the yeast and sugar to the warm milk.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients together, knead with kitchenaid.
  4. Place dough in greased bowl and let rise one hour or until doubled.
  5. Punch dough down. Cut dough into 8 peices.
  6. Cover the 8 balls and let rest for 15 minutes.
  7. Fill each flattened dough peice with leftover curry. Pinch the seam. Fold over seam and pinch again.
  8. Dip in egg and panko. Cover and let rise for 45-60 minutes or until 1.5 times its size.
  9. Deep fry with seam side down, then flip.
