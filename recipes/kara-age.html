Description

Japanese fried chicken marinated in soysauce, garlic, ginger, and egg.

Great for bentos. My kids love this recipe. Keeps well in freezer.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken (dark meat).
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Potato starch for coating
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 knob ginger
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • Green onion and lemon as garnich

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized peices.
  2. Place chicken in a bowl and marinate with salt, pepper, soy sauce, egg, grated ginger and garlic.
  3. Massage meat into mixture with hands and let it marinate overnight.
  4. Dip chicken peices in potato starch and deep fry.
  5. Garnish with a slice of lemon and green onion.
