Thai Coconut Curry

Description

Gluten free Thai chicken curry made in one skillet. It's ready in 20 minutes!

Curry is the best, eat more curry. This recipe can be made mild or hot.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Chicken breasts
  • 5 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp Ground coriander
  • 1 can Canned coconut milk
  • 1.5 cups Shredded carrots
  • 1-3 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • handful Fresh spinach
  • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • 1/4 cup Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, add the oil, diced onion, and saut√© over medium-high heat about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken and cook for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic, ginger, coriander, and cook for about 1 minute.
  4. Add the coconut milk, carrots, Thai curry paste, salt, pepper, and stir to combine. Reduce the heat and allow mixture to gently boil for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the spinach, lime juice, and stir to combine. Cook until spinach has wilted and is tender.
  6. Evenly sprinkle with the cilantro. Will keep airtight in the fridge for up to 1 week.
